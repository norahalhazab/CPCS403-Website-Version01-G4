FAQ

Important information before booking.

What is the cancellation policy?

Free cancellation up to 24 hours before the activity start time. Cancellations made less than 24 hours prior may not be refunded. In case of weather conditions or safety concerns, activities may be rescheduled or fully refunded.

Are there age restrictions?

Age requirements vary depending on the activity:

• Water activities: Minimum age 8 years (children under 12 must be accompanied by an adult).
• Diving: Minimum age 12 years.
• Desert hiking & e-biking: Minimum age 10 years.

All participants must follow safety instructions provided by the guide.
